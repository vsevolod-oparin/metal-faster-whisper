## Changes

- **MWTranscriber.h** — added `buildPromptWithPreviousTokens:withoutTimestamps:prefix:hotwords:` and `buildSuppressedTokens:`

  - Previous tokens truncated to `maxLength//2 - 1 = 223`
  - Hotwords and prefix encoded via tokenizer with space prepended
  - Suppression: -1 expands to model's non-speech tokens + 6 always-suppressed special tokens, sorted and deduplicated
